# Stay Safe Online - Prevention Checklist
## For College Students in India

---

1. ✅ Always check the sender's email address carefully
   before clicking any link - fake emails often have
   slightly altered addresses like support@amaz0n.com

2. ✅ Never click on links in unsolicited emails -
   instead go directly to the official website by
   typing the URL in your browser

3. ✅ Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on all
   important accounts including email, GitHub, and LinkedIn

4. ✅ Never share your OTP, password, or PIN with
   anyone - not even someone claiming to be from
   your bank or college

5. ✅ Use strong, unique passwords for each platform
   - a mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols

6. ✅ Always check for HTTPS and a padlock icon in
   the browser address bar before entering any
   personal details on a website

7. ✅ Keep your phone, apps, and browser updated
   regularly - updates fix security vulnerabilities

8. ✅ [UPI Safety] Never scan a QR code sent by a
   stranger - QR codes can initiate payments that
   debit money from your account directly

9. ✅ [UPI Safety] Your UPI PIN is NEVER required to
   RECEIVE money - if someone asks for your PIN to
   send you money, it is a scam

10. ✅ If you suspect any cyber fraud or online scam,
    immediately report it at:
    Website: cybercrime.gov.in
    Helpline: 1930 (available 24x7)

---

## Quick Rule to Remember
STOP - THINK - VERIFY
Before clicking any link or sharing any information,
stop for 30 seconds, think if it seems suspicious,
and verify through official channels.
